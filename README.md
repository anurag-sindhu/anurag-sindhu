<details>
<summary><b>⚙️ Highlights</b></summary>

- **🕒 Daily Market Schedule**  
  The app starts working **daily when the market opens** and goes to sleep when the **market closes**, automating all tasks from buy orders to sell triggers.

- **💡 Systematic Buy on Dips Investment Plan**  
  The app smartly creates **Trigger/GTT** setups, leveraging a strategy to buy shares on dips.
  - **📉 Dynamic Dip Percentage**  
    Automatically calculates a **custom dip percentage** for each share based on several factors, ensuring precision in decision-making.
  - **🧠 Intelligent Trigger/GTT Creation**
    1. **Create Trigger/GTT** if any **new holding** enters the portfolio
    2. **Deletes all local and remote GTTs/Targets** if the holding is no longer found, ensuring no unwanted triggers
  - **🔄 Maintain Trigger Price Gap**  
    The app ensures there's always a set **gap between current price and configured Trigger/GTT price** for each share, optimizing entry points
  - **📦 Quantity Configuration**  
    Users can define **quantities** for each trade, and the app executes accordingly
  - **🔺 Auto-Quantity Increase**  
    Once a Trigger/GTT is hit, the app automatically **increases the number of shares** for next trigger, following your strategy to maximize gains
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>💼 Target/Sell Feature</b></summary>

- **💰 Selling is as Important as Buying**  
  The app provides dynamic options for selling shares at the right moment. Users can specify **how much percentage** they wish to sell after price reaches certain threshold.
  - **⏳ Daily Sell Trigger Creation**  
    Every day, the app creates a **sell order/Trigger** with user-defined sell percentage or system's **pre-defined percentage**, whichever is earlier.
    - **System's Pre-defined Percentage** calculated based on stock price:
      - ₹100 stock → ~13% threshold
      - ₹500 stock → ~12% threshold
      - Pattern continues for other price points
    - Designed to catch sudden upward jumps, book profits, or enable **Intraday**/**STBT** conversions
  - **📈 Selective Selling**  
    User can **choose specific shares** for sale actions, with above logic applying only to selected shares
  - **📝 Personal Notes**  
    Users can **add personal notes** to track selling rationale, ensuring planned exit strategies are never missed
  - **🔍 Handles Sell Precision**
    - Triggers evaluation when **target percentage** is **0.5% away** from current market price
    - Uses system's pre-defined strategy when **no target percentage** given, ensuring timely exits
</details>
